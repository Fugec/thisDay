About thisDay.

A free, interactive window into human history — one day at a time.

What is thisDay.info?

thisDay.info is a free, ad-supported historical events calendar that lets you explore what happened on any day throughout history. Browse wars, scientific discoveries, political milestones, famous births, and notable deaths — all organized by date and drawn from Wikipedia's extensive historical records.

Whether you're curious about what happened on your birthday, a student looking for historical context, or simply a history enthusiast, thisDay.info makes the past accessible and engaging. No registration required. No paywalls. Just history.

What You Can Do Here

Browse Any Date

Navigate month by month and click any day to see a full list of events, births, and deaths from throughout history.

Filter by Category

Narrow events by topic — War & Conflict, Science & Technology, Politics, Arts, and more.

Read In-Depth Posts

Our blog goes beyond the bullet points, with original articles that place historical events in their broader context.

Dark Mode

Toggle between light and dark themes for comfortable reading at any time of day.

Works on Any Device

Fully responsive design that works great on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. Install it as a PWA for offline access.

Wikipedia-Powered

All event data is sourced from the Wikimedia Foundation's API, ensuring reliable and well-documented historical records.

Our Data

All historical event data on thisDay.info is sourced from Wikipedia via the Wikimedia REST API. Content is provided under the CC BY-SA 4.0 license. Each event links directly to its Wikipedia article so you can verify facts and explore further.

Our original blog posts are written independently and represent our own editorial perspective on historical events. We strive for accuracy but encourage readers to consult primary sources for important decisions.
