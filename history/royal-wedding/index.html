What happened

Prince William and Catherine Middleton married at Westminster Abbey in London on April 29, 2011.

Scale

The ceremony drew more than 1,900 guests inside the abbey and a huge broadcast audience around the world.

Place

Westminster Abbey gave the ceremony a direct link to centuries of British coronations, funerals, and state rituals.

Public image

The wedding placed a modern royal couple inside a ceremony built from older monarchy, church, and state traditions.

Institution

The British Royal Family organized the ceremony as both a family wedding and a public constitutional spectacle.
